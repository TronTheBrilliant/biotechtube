# BiotechTube Launch Plan

## Product Hunt

**Title:** BiotechTube — The Bloomberg Terminal for Biotech, but Free

**Tagline:** Track 11,000+ biotech companies, drug pipelines, and $7.5T in market data

**Description:**

BiotechTube is a free, comprehensive intelligence platform for the global biotechnology industry. We track over 11,000 biotech companies across 30+ countries, providing real-time market data, drug pipeline analytics, funding round tracking, and FDA approval monitoring — all in one place.

Unlike expensive terminal subscriptions, BiotechTube is designed to be accessible to everyone: investors screening for the next breakout biotech stock, researchers exploring therapeutic landscapes, and company executives benchmarking against competitors. Our data covers 1,039 public companies representing $7.5T+ in combined market cap, with historical data spanning 35 years.

Key features include interactive market charts with sector and country breakdowns, detailed company profiles with stock price history and pipeline data, a funding radar tracking the latest biotech funding rounds, and upcoming industry event tracking. Every data point is updated daily, and the entire platform is free to use.

Whether you are a biotech veteran or just getting started, BiotechTube gives you the data edge the industry needs — without the price tag.
